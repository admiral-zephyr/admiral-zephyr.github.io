Vicarious Procrastination
   I managed to finish my Overwatch placements for season 14 and CTF season 2. In CTF, I placed in the 1800s. And placed 1600s in regular competitive. All of which was streamed to Twitch. Nothing special, just wanted the season rewards. Eventually, some day, I'll feel like grinding a season out and raising my ranking to at least gold. I've yet to try climbling to platinum rank, I'm a little too casual to dedicate that much time to a serious practice regime. Especially since I don't have a full team of regular Overwatch players. But, this round of placements are finished.
   Ashe is quickly becoming one of my favorites. When she was first released, I played her in practice mode to get a feel for her but wasn't really in the mood for Overwatch. Since I wasn't too concerned where I landed in placements this season, I gave her a pick and hit the ground running. Coming from McCree, reloading the Viper threw me off a little bit but I quickly got used to it. The coach gun immediately felt right, and using the dynamite or B.O.B. was easy enough to pick up on. I didn't see many people pick her in my placements so I presume she's a very situational hero.
