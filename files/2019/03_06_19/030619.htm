Dubious Duty and Degenerate Debauchery
   So, I finally did it... I signed up for Google Fi. They had a Moto G6 on discount for $100 and my current phone was nearing the end of its life. Google offers $20 for unlimited calls and texts, and $10 per gigabyte and after 6 gigs it caps the charges. I should never have a bill greater than $80, in theory... We'll see how that goes, you should always be cautious signing up for anything.
   My first jury duty is turning out fine so far. All new to me, seeing this firsthand is pretty enlightening as to how our judicial system operates from within. Like any government system, there's a lot of bureaucracy or red tape involved. The day of, I showed up 30 minutes early, just to make sure I had plenty of time to roam around the courthouse. From there, I waited in line with about a hundred other potential jurors so we could pass the security checkpoint on the way in. Paperwork and registration followed. All of this took about an hour, and another hour waiting in the courtroom for further instructions. Nothing exactly interesting in itself, but it was neat to see how it operates.
   There's been an influx of AI Neural Network news going on for a while now. The names vary depending on the use, ESRGAN or Waifu2x, but this is becoming more and more amazing everytime I find something new about it. Going down the rabbit hole, I ended up stumbling upon a handy little website that'll let you feed it an image and it will apply Waifu2x upscaling for you to download. waifu2x.udp.jp is a great alternative to setting up your own project with bits and pieces from github repositories. This new upscaling is coming along nicely but implementing it is still a little difficult and really not worth the reward yet. At least for me. Eventually, I'll probably have a project set up. Maybe upscaling ancient dos games, like Betrayal at Krondor or something.
   So far, I've been upscaling tons of old wallpapers that were starting to show their resolution more noticeably. The Waifu2x upscaling is mostly just for anime style images right now and has little effect in other images. Other projects people are working on vary greatly, ranging from copying animations from a source file to decensoring hentai. It really is amazing to see this progress.
